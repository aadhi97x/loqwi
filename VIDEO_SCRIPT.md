# Loqwi — Video Walkthrough Script (≤3 min)

Record your screen (Edge/Chrome, mic enabled) running Loqwi at `http://localhost:3000` (or your deployed URL). Speak the bracketed lines aloud as you click/talk — they're both your narration *and* the actual voice commands the app will hear, so one take covers both.

Use **Smart Board mode** (top bar) before recording so on-screen text reads clearly on video.

---

### 0:00–0:15 — Cold open

*(Show the Welcome screen)*

> "Hi, I'm demoing **Loqwi** — a voice-first AI teaching assistant built for Option A of the CDF assignment: a hands-free co-pilot for a Hindi-English classroom on a smart board. It runs on Gemini, with the API key kept server-side, and it does four things hands-free."

### 0:15–0:50 — Concept Simplification

*(Click the mic, or just type/say it)*

> **"Samjhao photosynthesis."**

*(Wait for the response — point at the generated visual as it appears and Loqwi speaks the Hinglish explanation aloud)*

> "It generated a simplified Hinglish explanation, a step-by-step visual, and an everyday analogy — and it's reading it aloud right now, switching voices between Hindi and English mid-sentence so the Hinglish actually sounds natural."

### 0:50–1:35 — Voice-Triggered Quiz

> **"Quiz on fractions, paanch sawaal."**

*(Wait for the question to render and be read aloud)*

> **"B"** *(or whichever option — speak the letter to answer)*

*(Show the instant correct/incorrect feedback and score badge)*

> **"Next."**

> "It listens for the spoken answer, gives instant feedback with an explanation, tracks the score, and moves on with a voice command — no clicking needed."

### 1:35–2:10 — Bilingual Dictation & Translation

> **"Paudhe paani se khana banate hain, translate karo."**

*(Show the source panel and the translated panel filling in side-by-side)*

> "It transcribes what I said, and on the translate command, sends it to Gemini and shows — and speaks — the English translation right next to the original."

### 2:10–2:50 — Hands-Free Activity Guide

> **"Activity shuru karo addition ke liye."**

*(Show the generated step list, the countdown ring, and the current instruction being read aloud)*

> **"Pause."** … **"Resume."** … **"Next."**

> "This generates a short hands-on activity with timed steps. I can pause, resume, or skip steps with my voice while walking around the classroom — the timer and instructions update on the board without me touching anything."

### 2:50–3:00 — Close

> "That's all four required features, Hinglish throughout, with the Gemini key never exposed to the browser. Thanks for watching!"

---

## Tips for a clean take

- If a voice command misfires (classroom mic noise, accent drift), the **typed command box** at the bottom is a no-fail backup — use it if you're short on time rather than re-recording.
- Keep each spoken command short and natural; Loqwi's local intent router is tuned for phrasings like the ones above.
- If you want a calmer pace, **Hands-free mode** (toggle next to the mic) lets you say "Hey Loqwi" once and never touch the screen for the rest of the take.
